Hot Honey Wings

Description

These Detroit-inspired hot honey wings are packed with bold, finger-licking, sweet-spicy flavors. They'll be a hit at your next barbecue or game-day gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill to medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Pat wings dry with paper towels. Season wings with cayenne, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook chicken wings on the preheated grill, brushing wings liberally with 1/2 cup honey while cooking, until cooked through and juices run clear, 20 to 30 minutes.
  4. Mix butter, hot sauce, and remaining 1/2 cup honey together in a large bowl.
  5. Remove wings from the grill and immediately toss them in hot honey-butter sauce to coat. Serve wings or return to the grill for 1 minute per side to set sauce.
